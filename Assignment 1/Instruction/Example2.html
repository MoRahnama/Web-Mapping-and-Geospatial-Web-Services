UNIVERSITY OF NEW BRUNSWICK

The University of New Brunswick is located in New Brunswick’s capital city Fredericton. Its Saint John campus, is located in New Brunswick’s largest city.

UNB was established in 1785. It is among the oldest public universities in North America and the oldest English-language university in Canada.
